My name is Caroline Nowak. I am a junior at Tufts University majoring in Economics and minoring in Computers Science. Originally from Chicago, but as a person who loves to travel and experience new things, I am open to relocating both nationally and internationally.

My responsibilities included developing use cases through primary research and design-thinking methodology, and analyzing demand for big-data text analytics. Our assignment was to create a viable and marketable application for financial risk managers and analysts. The solution we developed met all key performance metrics by: 1) Successfully applying IBM Watson annotators to SEC documents to extract and analyze top threats; 2) Correlate entities at risk of particular threats; 3) Identify trends in these threats and their effects over time on a company, sector, and market level. We delivered a patentable product against tight deadlines.
